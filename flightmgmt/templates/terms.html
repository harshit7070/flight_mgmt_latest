1. Booking and Ticketing

  • The passenger's name on the booking must match the name on the government-issued ID that will be presented at check-in.
  • A confirmed booking is subject to the passenger providing valid identification documents at the time of check-in.
  • E-tickets are non-transferable and can only be used by the passenger named on the ticket.
  • The passenger is responsible for ensuring that all information provided during booking is accurate and complete.
  • Bookings are only confirmed once full payment has been received and processed.

2. Fare Rules and Payment

All fares are subject to the following conditions:

  • All quoted fares are in the specified currency and include all taxes and fees unless otherwise stated.
  • Fares may vary based on availability, seasonality, and booking conditions.
  • Payment must be made in full at the time of booking.
  • We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and other payment methods as specified during the booking process.
  • Additional charges may apply for certain payment methods.

3. Changes, Cancellations, and Refunds

  • Changes to bookings may be permitted as per the fare rules of your ticket, and may incur additional fees.
  • Cancellation fees and refund policies vary depending on the fare type purchased.
  • Non-refundable tickets will not be refunded in case of cancellation.
  • Refunds, when applicable, will be processed through the original payment method.
  • No-shows (failure to check in for your flight without prior cancellation) typically result in forfeiture of the ticket value.

4. Check-in and Boarding

  • Passengers must check in within the specified time frame before departure.
  • Online check-in is available from 24 hours up to 1 hour before scheduled departure.
  • Airport check-in counters close 60 minutes before scheduled departure for domestic flights and 90 minutes for international flights.
  • Boarding gates close 20 minutes before departure.
  • Failure to complete check-in and present yourself at the boarding gate on time may result in denied boarding with no refund.

5. Baggage Allowance

  • Baggage allowances vary based on route, fare type, and membership status.
  • Checked baggage typically includes 1 piece of up to 23kg (50lbs) for economy class passengers.
  • Carry-on baggage is limited to 1 piece not exceeding 7kg (15lbs) with maximum dimensions of 55cm x 40cm x 20cm.
  • Additional or overweight baggage is subject to excess baggage fees.
  • The airline reserves the right to refuse carriage of dangerous or prohibited items.

6. Special Assistance

  • Requests for special assistance, including wheelchair assistance, should be made at the time of booking.
  • Special meal requests must be made at least 24 hours before scheduled departure.
  • Medical equipment may be carried subject to prior approval and airline policies.
  • Passengers with reduced mobility are advised to arrive at the airport at least 2 hours before departure.

  • The airline is not liable for indirect, consequential, or special damages.
  • Claims for damaged baggage must be reported within 7 days of receipt; for delayed baggage, within 21 days of delivery.
